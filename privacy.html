Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 14, 2026

Introduction

Offgrid Training ("the app") is a workout tracker for iOS designed with privacy as a core principle. This Privacy Policy explains what data the app stores, how that data is handled, and what rights you have over your information.

The app is designed to function entirely offline and does not collect, transmit, or share your personal data with anyone.

What Data the App Stores

The app stores workout-related data that you create within the app. This may include:

  • Exercises, including names, categories, and variations
  • Workout logs, including sets, reps, weight, duration, distance, and RPE
  • Workout templates
  • Personal records and progress data
  • App preferences and settings

All of this data is created by you and stored locally on your device.

Data Storage

All data created within the app is stored locally on your device. The app does not use remote servers, cloud databases, or any external storage services. Your workout data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export or transfer it.

No Account Required

The app does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number. You can use the app fully without identifying yourself in any way.

No Cloud Sync

The app does not sync data to any cloud service. There is no server-side component and no automatic data transfer between devices. Your data exists only on the device where it was created, unless you manually export or transfer it.

No Advertising SDKs

The app does not include any advertising frameworks or SDKs. No ad networks have access to your data or your device through this app. The app does not display ads of any kind.

No Analytics or Tracking

The app does not use any analytics services, crash reporting tools, or third-party tracking technologies. No usage data, behavioral data, or device information is collected or transmitted. Your activity within the app is not monitored, recorded, or shared.

Data Export and Transfer

The app provides features that allow you to export your data, create backups, restore from backups, and transfer data between devices. When you use these features, you are in full control of where your data goes. The developer does not have access to your exported data.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children. Since the app does not collect personal data at all, no age-related data practices apply.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
