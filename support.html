Frequently Asked Questions

How is my data stored?

All your workout data is stored locally on your device. The app does not use cloud servers, remote databases, or any external storage. Your data never leaves your phone unless you explicitly export or transfer it.

Can I back up my data?

Yes. The app includes a backup feature that lets you create a full backup of your workout data. You can store this backup file wherever you choose and restore from it at any time.

Can I transfer my data to a new device?

Yes. You can export your data on your current device and import it on your new device. The app provides a straightforward transfer flow so you don't lose your training history.

Can I export my workouts?

Yes. You can export your workout data and templates from within the app. You can also share workout templates with others.

Does the app require an internet connection?

No. Offgrid Training is designed to work fully offline. No internet connection is needed to log workouts, view your history, or use any feature of the app.

How do I report a bug?

Send an email to support@example.com with a description of the issue. Include details like what you were doing when the issue occurred and your iOS version — this helps us investigate and fix the problem faster.
