Support

Help, common questions, and how to get in touch.

I built Offgrid Training primarily for myself — I wanted a workout tracker that works fully offline, with no accounts and no unnecessary bloat. But if you use the app and have questions, feature ideas, or find a bug, I'd genuinely like to hear about it.

Common questions

How do I back up my data?

Open the app's settings and use the backup feature to create a full backup file. This file contains all your workouts, exercises, templates, and settings. Save it somewhere safe — like your Files app, or send it to yourself via email or AirDrop.

How do I restore a backup or transfer to a new device?

On your new device, install Offgrid Training and go to settings. Use the restore feature and select your backup file. All your data will be imported. This is also how you move your data from one device to another.

Where is my data stored?

All your training data is stored locally on your device. Nothing is uploaded to any server or cloud service. If you delete the app, the data is removed with it — so make sure to create a backup first if you want to keep it.

How do I report a bug?

Send an email describing what happened, what you expected, and which device and iOS version you're using. Screenshots are helpful if you can include them.
